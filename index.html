We Love Capybaras

What are capybaras?

Capybaras are large semiaquatic South American rodents. Capybaras are the largest living rodents in the world and are primarily found in South America.

What do capybaras eat?

Capybaras are vegetarians and they eat between 3% to 4% of its bodyweight(around 6 to 8 lbs). Some foods include:

  • Sedges
  • Aquatic Plants
  • Switchgrass
  • Hay
  • Bark

Fun Fatcs About Capybaras

  • Capybaras eat their own poop for beneficial purposes as it provides a lot of protein
  • Capybaras can sleep underwater
  • Their teeth never stop growing!
  • Capybaras live among large herds of 10,20, as they are always around different kinds of animals
